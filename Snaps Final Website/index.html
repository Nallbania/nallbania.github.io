Welcome to Snaps!

An interactive mindfulness app meant to simplify gratitude for the Wellesley student
Learn More

We found that many students lack the time to practice gratitude or are overwhelmed by mindfulness apps that are too structured. So we designed an easy to use app that allows the user to maximize their time and personalize the structure of their gratitude practice
